How to turn off "find your iPhone" on your Computer

  1. Open any browser available on your Computer and go to https://www.icloud.com
  2. You will then ask to enter your Apple ID and Password then click the right arrow to login.

  3. On the dashboard, click the Find iPhone icon.

  4. Click All Devices, and click the iphone you want to turn off find my iPhone.
    Note: In order to remove the device on icloud, you need to turn off the device first, otherwise you won't be able to remove it.

  5. Click Remove from Account, and you will then asked for confirmation and click Remove.

  6. Great! you have now turned off the "Find my iPhone"
